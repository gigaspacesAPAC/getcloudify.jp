Intro to TOSCA LMS

Cloud orchestration is radically changing the efficiency of cloud computing - and TOSCA is standardizing how this is done.

Cloudify, with its unique open source cloud orchestration, has taken on the role of providing in depth training aimed at cloud architects and application developers interested learning more about how to implement TOSCA into their cloud deployments.

What will I learn?

  • Intro to TOSCA
    • Fundamentals of TOSCA Orchestration
    • How TOSCA is implemented in Cloudify

What is the first course comprised of?

  • The course contains four modules:
    • Two Theoretical Modules
    • Two Hands-on Labs Modules

Access to a Live Virtual OpenStack Lab

Our experts will guide you through a interactive OpenStack lab, and work with you on how to properly setup your cloud orchestration.
