Cloudify + vRealize Automation™ for Enterprises

Integrating Cloudify and vRealize Automation™ for Enterprise IT workloads is a match made in heaven. Only by combining the two together can you reap the rewards and capabilities of both of these industry leading powerhouses in the same Cloud infrastructure. The enterprise grade cloud management features of vRealize Automation™ working in harmony with the developer agility and technology agnostic Cloudify are the optimal solution for Enterprises who want to have one holistic Cloud solution for their Production, development and DevOps workloads together.

  • Extend your Enterprise IT cloud with this combination to offer Multi VIM support with ESXi and Openstack, and container support, all from the same orchestration engine.
  • Drag and drop service modelling and along with the ‘out of the box’ service catalog from vRealize Automation is made even stronger with Cloudify extensions for TOSCA and Yang/Netconf and HOT. Now you can orchestrate workloads and create service stacks that were never possible before, all with a simple unified management interface.
  • Monitoring and management support for the entire cloud infrastructure is all rolled up in to vRealize Operations Manager, regardless of VIM. Add on top of that auto-scaling and self-healing capabilities and that is one powerful monitoring solution!
  • Extend the ability to orchestrate more than just NSX™ as your SDN controller. With this combination you’ll have the ability to integrate and orchestrate Open DayLight, SD-WAN capabilities and more to create a truly integrated Enterprise grade SDN overlay network.
  • Out of the box cost and show-back capabilities for the entire Cloud to keep costs in line with the business objectives.
  • Integrated approval workflows to speed time to deployment of new infrastructures grant the rapid deployment with governance controls to ensure only the appropriate workloads are deployed.
  • Streamline Dev deployments in to Production through managed automated workflows that can push workloads to public or private clouds of your choosing.

It really doesn’t get any better than this for Enterprise IT workloads!
